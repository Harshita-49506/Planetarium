Our Mission

To Make Space Fun for Everyone

Welcome to Planetarium! We believe that learning about the universe shouldn't be boring. Our goal is to create a magical place where kids and their parents can explore the wonders of space together, through interactive games, exciting stories, and beautiful visuals. From the smallest asteroid to the largest galaxy, there's a universe of adventure waiting for you!

Meet Your Guide!

This is Amayra! She's our friendly and curious guide who will be with you on your journey through the cosmos. Amayra loves asking questions, discovering new things, and sharing the amazing secrets of space. Keep an eye out for her tips and fun facts throughout the site!

Our Philosophy

Learn
